Nano Swarms

Where:3D Printer
Cost:1000 Credits
Maximum Ammunition:2
the Nano Swarm appears in a grenade form. The player throws it on the ground and it will explode upon impact. It will create a nano sphere, and any zombies in it will be slowed down and damage will be inflicted on the zombies every one frame per second.

The Nano Swarm can be extremely useful when being swarmed by zombies, and can be used to escape. Due to its large radius, it can cover an entire path, and any zombie going after the player will be caught in. The Nano Swarm deals large damage and will easily kill zombies even near round 30. As the player is not affected by the swarm, it can be used to revive teammates, by throwing one close to any player.

Distraction Drone

Where:3D Printer
Cost:1000 Credits
Maximum Ammunition:2
it will immediately deploy on the ground after being thrown. The drone will then emit an extremely loud siren and it will attract every zombie in the map. It will explode in five to six seconds.

The Distraction Drone is often thrown to allow the player(s) to reload, retrieve power-ups, or revive downed players. Drones are also replenished by a Full Reload power-up. Therefore, throwing one to retrieve one is not a waste. Another good strategy is to get close to the horde of zombies around the Drone and either throw grenades or fire powerful weapons, which will quickly kill all of the zombies. More points can be obtained if done with a bullet firing weapon, such as an Ameli or a Pytaek.

A smart thing to do is if there is a Full Reload power-up is the player should waste their two Drones, because they will be replenished when the power-up is obtained. However, a Distraction Drone will not be given if one is being primed to throw. Alternatively, one is refilled at the beginning of each round if the player has less than the maximum amount of 2.
