Resupply

It is dropped by players upon death. When picked up, the player will obtain one of their dropped weapons. Only the player that died may pick a Resupply power-up.

DNA Bomb

Zombies will drop this. When activated, it will produce a green flash, and every Zombie in the map will be killed.

Hyper Damage

It is droped by Zombies. When activated, it will allow you to hit zombies with one punch or bullet.

Multiplier

Droped by Zombies. When activated, it will double the credits you get.

If a zombie is shot and gives you 10 credits. Then you will get 20 credits when Power Up is activated.

Security

Droped by zombies. When activated, it will activate every trap on the map. Doors, laser, all off them.
If you picked up the Power Up. It will give you credits every time a zombie is getting hurt by the trap. Other Players will not get the credits to. Only the person who pick it up will get the credits.

*Caution*The traps can kill you to.

Full Reload

Droped by zombies. When activated it will give you max ammo.

*Note* it will not include the mag you have in your gun.
The Power Up will drop at the end of every Infected round or Dog round.

Power Surge

Droped by zombies. When activated, all the 3D Printers will be turned on and will also cost 10 Credits.
